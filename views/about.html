Max Verstappen

Max Emilian Verstappen, the world-renowned race car driver, was born on September 30, 1997. He is a Belgian-Dutch racing driver and the ‘2021 Formula One World Champion.’ Under the Dutch flag, Verstappen competes in Formula One for Red Bull Racing. He is the son of Jos Verstappen, a former Formula One driver. At the 2015 Australian Grand Prix, he made Formula One history by competing as the youngest driver ever at age 17. Verstappen has 23 victories and 14 pole positions as of the 2022 Miami Grand Prix. Verstappen is one shining light that deserves to be recognized.
